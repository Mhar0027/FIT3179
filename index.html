Statistics Of Malaysian States w/ COVID-19 Data

Visualisations By Mikhail Haris

The map outlines the population per state of all Malaysian states. There is clearly a larger outline of population influx in Western Malaysia states instead of East Malaysia. Despite larger area density of some states, the population count varies throughout different states due to social, economic and developmental reasons.

The map outlines the population density per state of all Malaysian states as well as the area(squared km) of each outlined state. Despite the Eastern Malaysian states having a larger buildup area, the population density is still significantly higher in states located in West Malaysia.

Number of New Daily Cases from 2020-2021

The bar chart displays the number of new cases daily throughout Malaysia. A tooltip indicates the date, number of new deaths and total deaths. The use of a diverging color for the legend allows viewers to easily tell the increase or decreases in new daily cases.
